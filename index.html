About Kani

Kani's Whisk & Bakes started in a home kitchen in Chennai with a simple idea — bake the way family recipes taught her, with real butter, real chocolate and no shortcuts. Every brownie, cake and cookie here is still mixed, baked and packed by hand, in small batches, the same way it started.

No fancy factory, no preservatives — just a home baker who loves what she does, delivering fresh bakes to your doorstep across Chennai.
